Kung Pao Chicken

🍴12 Servings 🕓 15 Minute 🔥 565 Calories ★★★★☆ 4.0 Rating

Main Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast, cubed
  • Peanuts or cashews
  • Dried red chilies
  • Sichuan peppercorns
  • Garlic, ginger
  • Soy sauce, vinegar
  • Sugar, cornstarch
  • Vegetables: bell pepper, onion

Direction

Step 1: Marinate Chicken

  • Mix chicken with soy sauce, cornstarch, rice wine
  • Let sit for 10 minutes

Step 2: Make Sauce

  • Combine soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, cornstarch.
  • Add chili paste, Sichuan peppercorns.
  • Mix well, set aside.

Step 3: Stir-fry

  • Heat oil in wok until smoking hot.
  • Add dried chilies, stir briefly.
  • Add chicken, stir-fry until white (2-3 mins).
  • Add garlic, ginger, stir 30 seconds.
  • Add vegetables, cook 2 minutes.

Step 4: Combine

  • Pour sauce over chicken and vegetables.
  • Stir constantly until sauce thickens.
  • Add roasted peanuts.
  • Mix well

Step 5: Finish

  • Garnish with spring onions
  • Drizzle with sesame oil
  • Serve immediately with rice

NOTES

Kung Pao Chicken is one of the most famous Sichuan dishes known worldwide for its perfect balance of sweet, sour, and spicy flavors.
